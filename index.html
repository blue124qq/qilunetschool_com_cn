The Pigs


We're looking forward to welcoming you back for our reopening on Wednesday 2 December! We're taking bookings now and we ask that all our visitors make sure that they are following the latest government guidelines by being from just the one household when you eat with us. Thank you!

Since 2006 The Pigs has been the purveyor of real Norfolk food, and luxury accommodation (with a twist!).

The Pigs kitchen offers a seasonal menu with fortnightly changes. A creative and dedicated team serve real food without the pomp! Find less common cuts of meat served up in innovative and mouth watering dishes, a fifteen mile breakfast, iffits (Norfolk's answer to tapas), and down to earth puddings that promise to keep you sweet! Our ingredients are always fresh and as local as possible. We have a piglets menu just for the children.

You’ll feel like a pig in muck when you stay at The Pigs. In a good way, no actual mud. We have nineteen rooms available. Choose from Original Rooms, Spa Rooms, Spa Suites, and Dog Friendly.

A dedicated team ensure that your time at The Pigs is second to none. Passionate about making each guest happy you’ll find facilities to suite everyone. A family eating area, dining area, bar, a piggy play area, a luxury family suite, and for private dining there is Scandinorfia, and there’s even a pooch friendly menu!
