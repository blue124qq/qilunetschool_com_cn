BUBBLE AND SQUEAK

We have the perfect pad for a family of four.

As luxurious as any other of our rooms, and with plenty of space for you and your pigglets to stretch out, and relax.

Our pub offers a wonderful food and drink experience, and all the fun of our Piggleplay and indoor play areas will make sure all the family have a lovely stay. We feel we have created something really special, and a little out of the ordinary. Something that the whole family can enjoy and treasure.

Prices from £275.

A note for piglets - As well as a yummy piglets menu and family dining area, we also have plenty of fun spaces for you to roam about and let off steam.

Piggleplay will go down a treat with it's zip-wire, climbing wall, lego stations, shuffle board, table football, pool table, retro arcade games, and dig for a pig! Wowza- that's a lot of things to keep you busy!
