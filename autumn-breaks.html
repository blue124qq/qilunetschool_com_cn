Autumn Breaks

DECEMBER 2020


Two-night bed & breakfast getaway including dinner on the first night and 20% off PIGSPA treatments!

Long lazy days can be enjoyed taking advantage of your in-room facilities including a sauna (original and suites) and steam room (suites only), or a cosy up by the fire pit in your own private courtyard.

This autumn we're treating you to 20% off all our PIGSPA treatments, so you really don't need to go outside. Our therapists specialise in a variety of therapeutic massage treatments, indulgent body treatments, and restorative facials; you'll be spoilt for choice. With private treatment rooms to escape to, chose from a single treatment or double treatments for you and yours can enjoy together.

To book your two-night autumn break, call our friendly team on 01263 587634 or book online.

Norfolk is glorious in autumn, with large, sandy beaches perfect for blowing away the cobwebs and exploring some of the National Trust properties, right on our doorstep here at The Pigs.

Be sure to stop by our Spa Bar to pick up some PIGSPA products to use during your stay and purchase a little momento to enjoy The Pigs at home.
