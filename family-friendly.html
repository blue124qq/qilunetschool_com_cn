We're Family Friendly

Why should the little ones miss out on all the fun?

There’s a good reason why we are one of the most popular pubs in Norfolk for children:

Piggleplay - In 2011 we commissioned the creators of the wonderful tree house adventure attraction, BeWILDerwood, to build us a miniature version at the Pub. Piggleplay features slides, a Tree house, zip wire, climbing wall, maze, obstacle course and den building.

Indoor Play - No problems if the weather is poor, we have an adequate indoor play area with lego, brio train track, cars, kitchen, books, chalk boards, pool table, table football and The Pigs shuffle board. In addition colouring on back of menus and various board games to keep a range of ages happy.

Genuine Children’s Food - The food’s not bad either! Proper children’s food (Note to parents – we have good options on the vegetable side of things). As well as a family friendly dining area.

Family Rooms - We also have a family room available, which will accommodate up to 4. On top of this - it happens to be one of our original spa rooms too! The whole family can relax together at The Pigs.
