Summer Escapes

JULY - SEPTEMBER 2021


Two-night bed & breakfast getaway including dinner on the first night AND save 20% on all spa treatments!

Enjoy a perfectly designed bedroom, complete with a beautifully comfy bed, a private courtyard and spa area. Wake up to an amazing Pigs breakfast each morning, a scrumptious dinner and 20% off all spa treatments thrown in for good measure.

Long lazy summer days are meant to be spent at The Pigs. Indulge yourself in our in-room facilities by wallowing in your own private sauna (original and suites) and steam room (suites only), and enjoy the remainder of the day with a cocktail in your hand.



Sunday – Thursday

Original Spa - £430
Spa Suite - £510

Friday – Sunday

Original Spa - £495
Spa Suite - £580

Book your summer break online, or call our friendly team on 01263 587634.

Treat yourself to something from the PIGSPA menu during your stay, choosing from a variety of therapeutic massages, indulgent body treatments and restorative facials available with the PIGSPA therapists. Private treatment rooms let you chose from a single or double treatments for you and yours to enjoy together.

Don't forget to stop by the Spa Bar to pick up some PIGSPA products to use during your stay and treat yourself to a keepsake from our great selection of products you can purchase to take home.

Gorgeous vast skies, stunning countryside and coasts, as well as historical British market towns and quaint villages - just a few things that make Norfolk the perfect place to relax and unwind this summer. Be sure to take advantage of our fantastic summer break deals.
