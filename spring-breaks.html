Get out and be inspired by the stunning north Norfolk countryside this spring. Our two-night getaway allows you the best of both worlds offering you a comfortable base to sleep, eat and drink whilst allowing you to explore the beautiful places Norfolk has to offer. Our Original Spa rooms are dog friendly too!

Treat yourself to something from our Spa Treatments menu during your two-night spring getaway with a 20% discount. We have a variety of therapeutic massage treatments, indulgent body treatments, and restorative facials available with our exceptional PIGSPA therapists. We have separate treatment rooms where you can escape to, chose from a single treatment or double treatments for you and yours to enjoy together.

All rooms in this offer have a sauna and fire pit, and we can't think of a better way to spend an evening after a long day out and tasty dinner.

Be sure to stop by our Spa Bar to pick up some PIGSPA products for use during your stay and we have a great selection of products for you to take home.
