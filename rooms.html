Luxury Bedrooms

We have nineteen superbly appointed bedrooms at The Pigs.

In accordance with the latest government guidelines we will be closed between Thursday 5 November and Wednesday 2 December. Please call our team on 01263 711400 to move your booking or cancel your booking with no penalty charge. Rooms are booking up fast, so have a few dates in mind for your future stay with us; we can't wait to welcome you to The Pigs.

We will be taking new bookings throughout lockdown for dates from Wednesday 2 December onwards, including our autumn breaks and winter warmer offers subject to availability.

You’ll find a variety of luxury rooms at The Pigs. Each room has a rustic country style combined with modern day technology and quirky touches of Piggy luxury. Whichever room you pick you’re guaranteed a look and feel that will be remembered!

We’re conveniently located in the North Norfolk countryside just 10 minutes from the coast and a 30 minute drive from Norwich. Once you’ve found us you’ll be able to enjoy refreshment and food in The Pigs bar and restaurant area. Our seasonal, local menu will certainly tempt you. We’re real foodies here. Our menu is seasonal, creative, and as local as possible, and comes highly acclaimed!

USEFUL INFORMATION

Check in from 3pm, Check out is 11am

A large, free car park is at the front of The Pigs for all guests

Spa treatments must be booked ahead of your stay to avoid disappointment

Dog-friendly rooms are located on the ground floor in the Spa rooms. A surcharge of £25 per night will be applied

Spa Suites (rooms 11-19) can book a complementary 45-minute session in the Spa Garden for hot tub use. This is on a first come, first serve basis. Don't forget your swimwear!

We take a £50 deposit at time of booking with remaining balance paid on departure
