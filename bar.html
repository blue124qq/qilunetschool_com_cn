The passion that we have for great food continues on to The Pigs bar. You’ll find a range of local ales, wines, and special soft drinks suitable for grown ups and piglets alike!

We proudly present to you our ales from top East Anglian breweries. Woodfordes Wherry, Norfolk Brewhouse Moongazer, Adnams Bitter & Ghost Ship, and our very own Edgefield Old Spot brewed especially for us by Norfolk Brew House. We also have a gin shelf for any gin hounds out there. The Pigs wine list offers a wide variety too, the majority of which are served by the glass!

For drivers and non drinkers we recommend our homemade lemonade. It’s lip pursingly good! There are more soft drinks to tease you away from our beers. We have juices mixed by our bar pigs, and a number of nostalgic drinks available too. Why not try a Shirley Temple or Coke Float?

If you want to spend a couple of hours with some good company and a nice tipple there is no better spot than The Pigs bar. Find a comfy seat and whilst you sup your drink just try and resist our bar nibbles! Homemade Pork Scratchings, whole roasted & salted sweet corn, chilli flavoured broad beans & vegetable crisps. Perfect!

With friendly faces, real drinks, and tasty pig snacks, The Pigs bar has all the charm of a proper country pub.
